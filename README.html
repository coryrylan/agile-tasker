Agile Tasker

A small productivity Web App that simply is a countdown timer. The idea is to work in small sprints to help increase productivity and efficiency. I have found this invaluable in programming. Take 20 to 25 focused minutes on your problem. Then take a 5 min break no matter where you are in the problem. This will let you keep your mental edge and recharge.
